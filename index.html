Fusing R&B and Pop with a Message of Love and Inspiration
Meet Rehsa, a rising star in the South African music scene. With a fusion of R&B and pop, Rehsa creates music that revolves around the themes of love, heartbreak, and inspiration, drawing influence from artists like Chris Brown, Michael Jackson, and Brandy.
Rehsa has already achieved major milestones in his career, including signing with Def Jam Africa and receiving recognition from international artist Normani, who reposted one of Rehsa's mashup covers on Instagram. He has also recently released an EP called "The Colour" and has a school tour planned for 2023.
In addition to his solo work, Rehsa has also made notable collaborations with artists such as Bergie Fresh, appearing on his singles "Discard" (alongside Emtee) and "Save Me", as well as with St. Rush on the single "Fall Asleep."
Raised in Johannesburg in a household deeply rooted in music, Rehsa's parents exposed him to a wide range of genres from a young age, including reggae and gospel. However, he found his own way into the R&B world and has created a unique sound that incorporates his diverse influences
